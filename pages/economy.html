Economy Commands

Explore all commands for managing the in-game economy, from banking to rewards.

AdvancedBounty 1.0.1

  • /bounty list – View all active bounties.
  • /bounty set <player> <amount> – Place a bounty on another player.
  • /bounty claim – Claim a bounty reward after fulfilling a bounty.

BankUI 6.0.0

  • /bank open – Opens your bank UI.
  • /bank deposit <amount> – Deposit money into your bank account.
  • /bank withdraw <amount> – Withdraw money from your bank account.
  • /bank balance – Check your bank balance.

BedrockEconomy 4.0.4

  • /balance – Check your current balance.
  • /pay <player> <amount> – Transfer money to another player.

BetterRewards 2.0.0

  • /rewards – Open the rewards menu.
  • /rewards daily – Claim daily rewards.
  • /rewards weekly – Claim weekly rewards.
